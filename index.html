Texture is that little detail that is like the icing on the cake. Any strong texture converts very well to black and white and gives the image depth and realism. Clouds are my favorite texture for black and white photography, but there are other great textures like houses, hair, skin, and textiles. I like to create low-light macro photography with a focus on the texture of the subject. It can be eyelashes, fingers or the texture of a flower petal.
